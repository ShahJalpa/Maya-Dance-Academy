Our academy offers on-line dance lessons on bharatnatyam (Classical dance from india) for free. This is just a humble try for beginner students to understand the basic of Bharatnatyam. The main aim to provide useful information on bharatnatyam which is accessible online for global students. There will not be any student-teacher tradition. If you want online one on one based lessons with teacher, please fill out the form on contact page with your message and our Maya staff will contact you as soon as possible.

Bharatanataym

ORIGIN: State of Tamil Nadu in Siuth India
COMBINATION:
       Bha – Bhava – Expression
       Ra – Raga – Music
       Ta – Taala – Rhythm
       Natyam – Nritya – Dance
GHARANA:
       Pandanallur Style
       Tanjavur Style
       Maisur Style
       Chidambram Style
       Melattur Style
LANGUAGE:
Mainly Tamil, Telugu, Kannad, Sanskrit
In present days Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali and Hindi
MUSIC
Karnataki music which is called South Indian Music
