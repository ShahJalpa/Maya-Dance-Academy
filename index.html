WELCOME

Welcome to Maya Dance Academy! We have been teaching Indian dance in Chicago south-west sururb areas for 5 years and are proud to be the premier dance classes in the southwest. Our class is offering range from classical Bharatanatyam to energetic Bollywood dance classes for kids, teenage and adults in Tinley Park, IL. We also offer private lessons for weddings and other events. We guarantee we will find a perfect class for you, so come check out one of the most unique dance class!

MAYA MOTIVE

MAYA's main motive is to educate student in dancce with the confidence. We expect our student to achieve the stage of perfection that makes him/her a complete dance artist.

In other words, MAYA looks for authorize students, artists and audiences with the gear to be inspired, interested, positive and sympathetic in their regular interface with the world through fineness in all kind of dance teaching and performance.

NATYA KARMA

"Yato hasta stato drishti
                            Yato drishti stato manah
Yato manah stato bhava
                            Yato bhava stato rasaha"
                                                        - Nandikehswara

Meaning

Where the hand is, eyes should follow there
Where the eyes is, the mind should be there
Where the mind is, there goes the expression,
And where there is expression, there arises emotion.
