Classical Dance

It is called "Natya Shastra". A Classical Dance means the reproduction of the natural behavior of men and women on the stage using some gestures, poses, face expression etc., on music and songs. So it is a series of theoretical movements.

Classical dances of India and their origin:
1. Bharatnatyam - Tamil Nadu
2. Kathak - Uttar Pradesh
3. Kathakali - Kerala
4. Kuchipudi - Andhra Pradesh
5. Mohiniattam - Kerala
6. Manipuri - Manipur
7. Odissi - Odissa

Folk Dance

When people get together and dance in joy to celebrate religious and social festival in an open area or ground, it is known as Falk Dance. Find more about folk dances in India, click here.

Modern Dance

There is no particular definition of Modern Dance. Modern dances are used in a movie. It is mixture of classical, folk, hip hop, jazz, salsa etc.

DANCE THERAPY

(Indian Perspective)

Dance provides an active, non-competitive form of exercise that has potential positive effects for physical health as well as mental and emotional wellbeing.
                                  - Chatterjee, 2013a

Dance therapy is specialized and psychotherapeutic use of dance for the improvement of cognitive, behavioral and physical conditions. Dance therapy is based on the idea that body and mind are co-relational, that the physical state of the body can affect the emotional and mental wellbeing both positively and negatively.
                                  - Quin, Redding & Frazer, 2007

Indian philosophy also supports the facts of Sangeet (song, dance and music) for benefit of human health physically as well as mentally.
                                  - Shastri, 1931
